Peanut Butter Balls

A simple, not-too-sweet treat for all ages!

Description

This is an easy recipe that everyone can enjoy. It's a fun one to bring in the kids and have them help, too! I shouldn't have
to say that this recipe contains peanuts, so look out for anyone with allergies. Otherwise, this is a treat that can make even the
coldest people smile.

Ingredients

    3/4 cup brown rice syrup 1/2 cup coconut oil 1 cup natural peanut butter 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 3 cups oats, or to taste 1 cup raisins 1/2 cup unsweetened flaked coconut

Steps

  1. Combine brown rice syrup and coconut oil together in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until melted and boiling 3 to 4
    minutes. Remove saucepan from heat.
  2. Mix peanut butter and vanilla extract into syrup mixture until smooth. Add oats, raisins, and coconut to peanut butter mixture
    and mix well. Refrigerate mixture until cool, at least 30 minutes. Form mixture into balls and freeze until set, at least 1 hour.
